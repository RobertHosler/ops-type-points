Source: Intro to instrinctual subtypes
The Dominant Instinct is a "weakness which looks like a strength" in that it is overdeveloped. The needs of this instinct are tended to, but there is an overfixation which results in a detriment to the individual. The Playground Instinct is a space that receives focus, but the stakes do not feel as high as in the Dominant. The second instinct is thus often the instinct which will cause us the least problems. The Blind Spot is the area we ignore and view as less important than the others. The needs of this instinct are often satisfied indirectly through strategies of the prior two instincts.
