Subjective Personality

Subjective Personality is a site dedicated to documenting and exploring concepts and theories of personality. Here at SP our team of writers aim to help make these sometimes complicated theories of personality more accessible and, dare I say, fun to learn about. While not ourselves experts in psychology, we feel that we can provide a service in accumulating the broad spectrum of information and giving you the good stuff in brief. Think of us as a friendly neighbor welcoming you to the typology community with a giggly jello dessert. Bon appetit!
